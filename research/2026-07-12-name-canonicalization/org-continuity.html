Do the same organizations carry across a category rename?

For each category that changed names, I matched the actual nonprofits funded (by tax ID / EIN) under the old name vs. the new name. High overlap = same category wearing a new label. Org detail exists FY2016 onward, so pre-FY2016 eras can't be tested this way. Overlap = share of the smaller side's orgs that also appear on the larger side.

Reference point: two genuinely different categories (Youth Services vs. Older Adult Services) share only 17% of orgs. So the high numbers below are meaningful, not just "everyone gets a bit of everything."
